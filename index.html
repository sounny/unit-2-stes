Annual Median Air Quality Index in USA Metro Areas

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is an aggregated term to evaluate air quality from multiple air contaminants.
The data for this map shows median AQI per year per metro area, as defined and tracked by the US Enironmental Protection Agency (EPA).

Metro areas are defined by EPA Core Based Statistical Area (CBSA).

The top 40 most populated metro areas are shown, with their Median AQI from years 2016-2021.

Data Source: EPA Air Data Annual Summary
